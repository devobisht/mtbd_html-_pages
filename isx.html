Sizes

Note that the below numbers are reference values only. The reality can differ depending on body ergonomics and body type. The chart below is designed to help finding the potential right size easier.

 

And this is how it is measured correctly:

A  Chest : the largest part of the chest measured horizontally.
B  Waist circumference: Measured loosely around the waist.
C  Hip circumference: strongest point measured around the hips / buttocks.
D Leg circumference:  measured approx. A hand's breadth above the middle of the knee.
E Shin length:  measured from the center of the knee to the ankle.
F Hand circumference: Palm  measured according to the thumb.
G Head circumference: start  approx. 2 cm above the ears.
H Arm circumference:  biceps circumference, measured approx. A full hand width above the elbow joint.
I Elle:  Measured from the middle of the elbow to the wrist-ankle.
